<objective>
Fix two coupled v1.1 bugs surfaced during operator UAT on Vantage Demo-10k:
  1. XAUUSD pip-size constant treats a *point* as a *pip* (10× off) across three
     independent locations, causing text-only signals to hit broker
     `order_check retcode=10016 Invalid stops` and percent-mode lot sizing to
     under-size by 10×.
  2. `_execute_open_on_account` has no `fixed_lot` branch — operator-configured
     fixed lot sizes never reach MT5 even though `stage_lot_size(snapshot)`
     correctly computes them and the value persists/displays everywhere else.

Root-cause analysis lives in `.planning/debug/text-only-stops-fixed-lot.md`
(status: `root_cause_found`); this plan is the implementation half.
