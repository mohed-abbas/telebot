<objective>
Implement EXEC2-06 (D2-01..D2-04, D2-14): rewrite standalone `_handle_open` (trade_manager.py:548-577) from the v1.0 single `zone_mid` full-fill into a multi-stage scale-in that **mirrors `_handle_correlated_followup`** (trade_manager.py:362-544) — the largest task in the phase.

Locked mechanics:
- **D2-01:** Route a standalone OPEN carrying zone+SL+TP through `compute_bands` → `create_staged_entries` (status `awaiting_zone`) → fire at-arrival bands → arm the rest; `_zone_watch_loop` fills the rest as price enters. **NO resting-limit orders** — reuse the zone-watcher to inherit D-21 kill-switch drain, D-14 pre-flight, D-24 reconnect reconcile for free.
- **D2-02:** **No forced market anchor.** Unlike the correlated path (whose stage 1 is the prior text-only market fill), the direct-zone path fires ONLY bands price has already crossed at arrival (`stage_is_in_zone_at_arrival`). If price is entirely outside the zone, **nothing fires** — all bands arm and wait.
- **D2-04:** `compute_bands` returns `[]` at `max_stages=1`. Do NOT fall back to `zone_mid`, and do NOT reuse the follow-up's `no_bands` branch (different semantics — RESEARCH Pitfall 5). Synthesize ONE whole-zone `Band(stage_number=1, low=zone_low, high=zone_high)` and run it through the same at-arrival/arm logic. Uniform model: every max_stages behaves the same.
- **D2-14:** A direct multi-stage OPEN that arrives after price ran past the zone is rejected by the existing `_check_stale` guard FIRST, before any band fires. Never chase a moved market. Per-band pre-flight re-check (D-14) is the second backstop.
- **signal_id** is the OPEN's OWN `log_signal` id (no `paired_signal_id`). Persist `signal_sl=signal.sl` and `signal_tp=signal.target_tp` on each created row (the columns from Plan 01) so the zone-watch read path (Plan 02) carries them.
- **SL-less guard:** the `signal.sl is None` early skip added in Plan 03 applies to this path too (it lives at the top of `_handle_open`).

This plan is **autonomous: false** — live-money staged scale-in against MT5 demo cannot run under DryRunConnector (deploy-at-end). DryRun tests gate the logic; a human-verify checkpoint gates live behavior at final VPS acceptance.

Purpose: standalone zone signals scale into the zone correctly and never chase a moved market.
Output: trade_manager.py `_handle_open` multi-stage rewrite.
</objective>
